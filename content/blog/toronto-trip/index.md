## 🗺️ Our Route

**August 11–23, 2026** – Toronto and Niagara Falls family trip with Leon and Emil

---

## Day 1 – Monday, August 11
Arrival in Toronto. Check in at the hotel – we're looking at Hilton or Marriott (Dani has a discount code, need to check!). Easy first day, explore the neighbourhood, grab dinner downtown.

## Day 2 – Tuesday, August 12
**Toronto Zoo & African Safari**

One of the largest zoos in the world – a full day for sure. Don't miss the African Savanna section. Bring snacks and comfortable shoes, the zoo is huge!

🎟️ Tip: Book tickets online in advance, or include it in the [Toronto CityPASS](https://www.citypass.com/toronto) to save money.

## Day 3 – Wednesday, August 13
**CN Tower & Ripley's Aquarium**

Start the morning at the **CN Tower** – the glass floor is a must, the kids will love (or hate) it! Right next door is **Ripley's Aquarium of Canada**, perfect for the afternoon. Both are included in the Toronto CityPASS.

## Day 4 – Thursday, August 14
**Toronto Island**

Take the ferry from Jack Layton Ferry Terminal to the Toronto Islands. Beaches, bike rentals, and a great view of the skyline. The water is calm and perfect for kids.

## Day 5 – Friday, August 15
**Africa Lion Safari**

About 1.5 hours from Toronto in Hamilton. Drive through the safari park with animals roaming freely around the car – always a highlight for the kids! Plan for a full day.

## Day 6–7 – Saturday–Sunday, August 16–17
**Free days / AirBnb on the sandbar**

Relaxing days at our AirBnb on the sandbar where the water is warm – perfect for the kids. Explore the area, swim, recharge.

## Day 8–9 – Monday–Tuesday, August 18–19
**Niagara Falls (1 overnight stay)**

About 1.5 hours from Toronto. Stay at a hotel with a view of the falls – absolutely worth it, especially at night when they're lit up! 

Things to do:
- Walk along the promenade
- Hornblower/Niagara City Cruises boat tour
- Journey Behind the Falls

## Day 10–12 – Wednesday–Friday, August 20–22
**Back in Toronto – loose ends**

Final days to catch anything we missed, do some shopping, or just enjoy the city at a slower pace.

## Day 13 – Saturday, August 23
Departure day. Head home with lots of great memories (and probably tired kids 😄).
